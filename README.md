# 🌍 India–US Trade & Tariff Analytics Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project explores international trade transactions between **India and the United States** using a structured trade dataset. The objective is to analyze trade flows, tariff impacts, logistics performance, and category-level insights through an interactive **Power BI dashboard**.

The analysis was conducted by carefully exploring the dataset and transforming the data into meaningful business insights using data visualization techniques.

---
🎯 Objectives
Analyze international trade patterns between India and the US
Understand tariff impact on different product categories
Track supplier and buyer performance
Evaluate logistics efficiency (transit time & shipment mode)
Identify top-performing categories and trade trends
---

# 📂 Dataset Overview

The dataset represents trade transactions with detailed attributes related to trade operations, pricing, logistics, and tariffs.
 * FY 2024–2025
 * FY 2025–2026

### Key Columns in the Dataset

* **transaction_id** – Unique trade transaction identifier
* **scenario_code** – Trade scenario between India and US
* **transaction_date** – Date of trade transaction
* **origin_country / destination_country** – Trade origin and destination
* **transaction_type** – Import or Export
* **buyer_id / supplier_id** – Trade participants
* **category_group / category_tier** – Product classification
* **quantity & unit_price** – Trade quantity and price
* **trade_value** – Total transaction value
* **shipment_mode** – Air or Sea transportation
* **transit_days** – Logistics delivery time
* **HSN_code / tariff rates** – Trade tariff classification

These attributes enable detailed trade analytics across multiple business dimensions.
